School Admissions made easy!

Benefits to Schools
  • Resource 80% reduction in workload and paperwork
  • Easy and quick access to applicant database
  • 24*7 updates about the applied candidates
  • Monitor the on-going process anywhere, anytime
  • Minimizes the time of processing applications
Benefits to Parents
  • Admission portal is open 24*7
  • Apply to schools right from the convenience of your home or office
  • Avoid standing in long queues
  • Receive updates about the admission status
  • Chances of making errors are minimal

Overview of the complete
Admissions process

  • Step 1: Search and Select

    • Search for schools by the area pin code
    • An overview of school details is provided
    • Select the school of your choice and proceed to apply

  • Step 2: Registration and Login

    • Register on Intelliadmissions.org admission portal using your mobile number
    • Login credentials will be sent via SMS
    • Login to the admission portal using the login credentials

  • Step 3: Application

    • Fill correct candidate details
    • Help Text provided for every step of application
    • Verify the details before submission

  • Step 4: Shortlisting and Document Verification

    • Shortlisting of candidates done by Schools
    • SMS is sent to all the shortlisted candidates
    • Document verification date and time details mentioned in the SMS as well as on the school website

  • Step 5: Selection and Fee Payment

    • SMS sent to parents of selected students
    • Parents can pay the school fees using our online fee payment portal

Why Intelliadmissions?

Features
  • System sets up criteria based on the school’s requirements
  • Automatic shortlisting of students
  • Integration of the admission portal on the school website
  • Student data available in pdf and doc format too
  • Dashboards available for monitoring and managing information
Benefits
  • Admission portal open 24*7
  • Less paperwork makes the admissions process environment friendly
  • Parents can apply to multiple schools using one admission form
  • Digital data management ensures easy access to student data
  • Real-time reports on the admissions process
Participate
  • Schools can enrol within a day
  • Admission portal is set up within 2-3 days
  • Software training provided to staff
